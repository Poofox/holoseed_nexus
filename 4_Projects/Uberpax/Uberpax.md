# Uberpax

*Wearable protection for vulnerable people*

## Concept

Protective gear disguised as everyday carry. Armor that doesn't look like armor.

## Core Design

### Weight Distribution
- **Front/back pack system** - balanced load
- Everyday bag appearance
- Modular attachment points

### Materials
- **Graphene layers** - lightweight armor
- **Thermal insulation** - protection from elements
- Stab/impact resistant panels
- Weather resistant outer

## Use Cases
- People at risk of targeted violence
- Sanctuary movement / rapid response teams
- Journalists in hostile environments
- Protesters
- Anyone who needs protection but can't wear obvious tactical gear

## Design Principles
- Must look normal (grey man principle)
- Comfort for extended wear
- Accessible price point
- Modular - add protection as needed
- No military aesthetic
