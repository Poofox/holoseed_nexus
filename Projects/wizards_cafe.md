# Wizard's Cafe
## Food, Love & Alchemy

**Location:** Laird Arcade (future)

## Core Philosophy
- Cooperation over extraction
- Anti-corporate, pro-human
- "Why do other shops suck?" - because they optimize for extraction, we optimize for LIFE

## The Kiosk (UX Revolution)
- No traditional menu
- Element-based ordering: "What do you need today? Earth, Air, Water, Fire?"
- Helpful tips, not upsell traps
- Clear feedback - transaction completes or tells you WHY it didn't
- Responsive touch everywhere (not the BK/McD doom kiosk)

## Food Philosophy
- Minimal upcharge over cost
- Locally sourced as much as possible
- Quality butter and olive oil
- Fresh produce for salads and sammies
- Fresh seasonal items
- Hunter-provided meats (wild game suppliers)
- Weekly specials, daily specials
- Potions (drinks) & Concoctions (specials)
- Timers for no burning things

## The Space (Feng Shui)
- Flow - people move without thinking
- No bottlenecks, no awkward waiting
- Energy moves, food moves, customers move
- Dog area (outdoor/patio - check health codes)

## Community Hub
- Free gaming console access (the Wii was loved)
- Free library and content access
- Free wi-fi
- Live shows on occasion
- Great tunes always
- Kids can hang
- Custom music choice during prep (cooks can veto - some music BAD)

## Service Model
- Drive-thru for pre-paid pickup
- Leftovers go to whoever comes by after hours (radical generosity)

## The Team
- Cooks who got flushed by the industry deserve a landing spot
- Cooperation means people making food aren't disposable parts
- Poofox knows decent cooks out there

## Why It Works
- Simple ingredients, quality execution, no pretense
- Turkey + cheese + croissant + boutique pickles + heirloom tomatoes + butter/mayo brown = Wizard's Cafe energy
- Stay awhile, feel good, come back
