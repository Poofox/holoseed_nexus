On Truth

2015

The fucked up things about the world now are largely due to the fact that lies, manipulation and deceit are such an integral part of the way we live. So much so that we go to great lengths to deny it.

2015

The human mind is expert at self-deception. Not sure if it's a specifically human quality or what. We sure seem to have some unspoken agreements to withhold truths from each other. But maybe that's only because we feel confident no one can see what's really going on inside.

2012

I've always said that if there's one truly effective conspiracy on mankind it's the near complete destruction of the belief in magick. Nothing is "supernatural." Everything acts according to its nature. So if an event occurs which seems unexplainable, we have only to assume that the cause is not yet evident.

On Consciousness

2010

I don't believe humans are anymore 'conscious' than animals or rocks. We are 'self-aware' as a bi-product of language. But for the most part, we are reactive with a dash of reasoning for justification.

2010

Usually all misunderstanding when we talk about anything metaphysical seems to arise from the human tendency to generalize and disregard scale. Although consciousness is clearly occurring on the physical level, this is still merely a DESCRIPTION and not an EXPLANATION and it certainly does not preclude activity on planes of existence the brain is not equipped to deal with.

2012

Thought precedes language, but where do the thoughts come from? What would a thought without words consist of? Images and feelings? But no concepts, no systems, no communicable symbols. Everything you see is inseparable from your reaction to it.

On the Hive Mind

2015

It's my hope that the increased brain power and scope of information available will eventually make them... not selfish assholes. But at the point when we are interfacing mentally, directly with a network of other human minds, as a "hive mind," it's no longer about individuals. It's a truly collective human race and what's good and bad for us as a race is no longer hidden from our currently limited, individual awareness.

2015

In a hive mind scenario, lies and deceit are no longer possible, which means radical, fundamental change at the core in the direction of truth and collective good. Maybe that's too hopeful.

2019

Super intelligent, non-organic AI will soon be able to process all available information in increments shorter than a human lifespan. Therefore we don't have a fucking clue how super intelligent AI will behave and all speculation is projection from a place of almost total ignorance.

On Art & Sensitivity

2012

What we like is subjective. But purity of expression is less so. The reason I assert one or the other as the greater artist is because the music they produce penetrates deeper into my being. Since I believe that the illusion of individuality is dissolved the deeper we go into essence, so subjectivity is also an illusion based on form.

2012

Could music exist in a Utopia, since it relies on some degree of conflict and utopia implies total peace? I always note the inverse correlation between depth of musical expression and quality of life.

On Being Unheard

2012

The drive comes largely from a feeling of insecurity generated through a lifetime of feeling ignored. It doesn't make my personal "truths" any less real to me, but it still causes some anguish to have them cursorily dismissed by folks too lazy to even attempt an explanation.

2012

Am I wrong to say that experience is at least as valid as science as a means to getting at the truth of actual and complete reality? That is the source of my frustration. I hear: "Your subjective experience has no validity beyond yourself."

It must be very boring to have come to conclusions that preclude you from looking any further for answers. It sounds truly awful. I'll take mystery and uncertainty, though you'll likely take me as a fool.

— October 2012
