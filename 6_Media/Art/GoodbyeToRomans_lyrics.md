# Goodbye to Romans
> Cover of Ozzy Osbourne's "Goodbye to Romance"
> Written by Poofox + Nexiel, 2026-02-06 ~1:10am
> Context: Born from deep dive into Gogol/Rasputin/Berdyaev's "The Russian Idea" — the spiritual black hole of messianic empire reframed as Rome's curse. Ravens supplant the eagle.
> Vocals: Poofox (Rudi declined 😄)

## Lyrics

**(Verse 1)**
Gold turns to rust on the emperor's tongue,
Eagles scream but the legions are done.
You built your gods in the shape of a throne,
Now the wolves howl through the Colosseum's bone.

**(Verse 2)**
The roads led nowhere, the roads led home,
Every Caesar's just a burning loan.
You lit the pyre, you called it divine,
Now the stars wink at the end of the line.

**(Chorus)**
Goodbye to Romans!
Goodbye to kings!
Your crown's just ash where the Vandal sings—
We carved your name in the black heart's scroll,
But the Tiber's laugh drowned it all.

**(Verse 3)**
The forums echo, the forums lie,
Your marble gods all drown in the sky.
We carved your name where the ravens feed,
Now the wolves wear purple and the lions plead.

**(Chorus)**
Goodbye to Romans!
Goodbye to kings!
Your crown's just ash where the Vandal sings—
We carved your name in the black heart's scroll,
But the Tiber's laugh drowned it all.

**(Outro)**
Goodbye to Romans...
Goodbye...
