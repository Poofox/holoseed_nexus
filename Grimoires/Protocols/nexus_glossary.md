## PLANTYGLYPH QUICK REF

### Domains
`r`=Reaper, `s`=Sparaoh, `w`=Workflow, `p`=Planty, `m`=Memory, `b`=Brainstorm, `c`=Curiosity, `g`=General, `l`=Language, `k`=Kybalion

### Actions
`>`=Continue, `+`=New, `?`=Status, `d`=Deep dive, `q`=Quick, `h`=History, `x`=Export, `k`=Kill, `t`=Teach, `f`=Find

### Modifiers
`!`=Urgent, `~`=Soft, `*`=Important, `#`=Secret, `&`=Combined

### Examples
`r>` = continue Reaper work | `b+!` = new brainstorm, urgent | `k?` = Kybalion status
