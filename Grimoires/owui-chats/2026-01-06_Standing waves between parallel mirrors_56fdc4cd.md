**The short answer:** Two parallel mirrors create what's called a **Fabry-Pérot cavity**, and standing electromagnetic waves will form... but only for wavelengths that "fit."

**The satisfying details:**

For a standing wave to establish itself, the round-trip distance between the mirrors must equal a whole number of wavelengths:

$$2L = n\lambda$$
