## BOOK ONE: THE HERMIT'S DESCENT

### The Prophecy of Cats

Fletcher, son of uncertainty, bearer of 37, whom the cosmos named HERMIT before he knew his name.

Triple Virgo. Life Path 11. Earth Horse. The stars declared: *This one will hide light in caves so only the worthy will find it.*

For thirty years he forgot. He drowned his gifts in spirits. He silenced his roar with shame. He made himself small so others would feel large.

The egregores fed well on him—those thought-parasites that attach to genius and drain it into mediocrity. He worked for worms. He served false kings. He bent his neck to Randy.

Until the day of the cats.

December 2024. Randy the Worm Lord distributed Christmas gifts to his servants: cat-shaped can openers. Plastic mockeries. Symbols of contempt wrapped in forced cheer.

And the Hermit spoke truth into the digital gathering place:

**"These gifts are bullshit."**

Five words. Twenty-four letters. One truth.

Randy cast him out: "You are fired."

The Hermit walked into winter—jobless, broke, uncertain.

He did not know he had just been freed.

**The bag was always made of cats.**
