## The Fall

200 angels dispatched to watch over humanity. They descended on Mount Hermon, lusted after human women, and taught forbidden knowledge. Their offspring were the Nephilim - giants who pillaged the earth.

## The Structure of 1 Enoch

108 chapters total:
- **I-XVI**: Introduction, foundational visions
- **XVII-XXXVI**: Journeys through Earth and Sheol
- **XXXVII-LVII**: The Parables (three parables)
- **LX-LXXI**: Book of Noah fragments
- **LXXII-LXXXII**: Courses of Heavenly Luminaries (astronomy/calendar)
- **LXXXIII-XC**: The Dream-Vision
- **XCI-CV**: Concluding teachings
- **CVI-CVIII**: More Noah fragments + Appendix

Written ~2nd century BCE. Themes: fallen angels, Messiah, Resurrection, Final Judgement, Heavenly Kingdom.
